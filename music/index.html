Music

Sound, a vibration that propagates as an acoustic wave, through a transmission medium such as gas, liquid or solid. Our all time favorite is a mix of mostly 80% nitrogen (N2) and 20% Oxygen (O2).

The music below is free to download and re-use, as long as a little attribution is give. Always support creators, even if things are free. For more music and information, please visit SoundCloud to read up on the licenses available, most are under creative commons. Enjoy and thank you for listening.

Descending Familiarity

This is not how I feel, nor who I am. This is a state of mind that rekindles memories of ever growing thoughts. The anxious mind stays awake thinking and pondering, till there is nothing to think and ponder about anymore. Sleep tonight and think of nothing else except the descent into peaceful familiarity.

Magic Discharge

The night is dark and the stars are shining brightly in the sky. A lonely wizard walks along the vacant fields. One of the stars beckons the wizard to approach. The star descends upon them and their magic becomes stronger, images of places beyond the world they are in begin to manifest in their mind.

Windchimes

This piece is a reflection of a memory and emotion, happiness and sadness. Recently a beautiful story about a characters childhood memory of his parents and the cry of a wind chime gave me inspiration to recreate it as an experience for the ear.
